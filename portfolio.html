About Mitch

Mitchell Marcus is a photographer, filmmaker, and web and media designer. Upon completion of his first documentary, “Javan: A Life in Football,” that followed the experiences and hardships of a semipro football player’s experience during a championship season, Mitch then shifted focus and passion towards other mediums, including photography, web media design, and audio engineering.
